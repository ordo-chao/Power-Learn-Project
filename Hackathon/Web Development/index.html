Havenwood Academy

Welcome to the illustrious halls of Havenwood Academy, nestled in the quaint town of Evergreen Springs, founded in 1892. This esteemed institution has been a beacon of academic excellence and character development for over a century. Countless luminaries have passed through its doors, shaping the course of history with their intellect and ambition. Among its distinguished alumni is Clara Montgomery, the renowned scientist whose groundbreaking research in renewable energy revolutionized the field. Not to be forgotten is Marcus Thompson, the celebrated author whose literary works have inspired generations. Havenwood Academy's commitment to nurturing talent and fostering innovation continues to draw the brightest minds from across the globe, ensuring its legacy as a bastion of learning and achievement for years to come.
